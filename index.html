About Ohio Families CANN

Ohio Families CANN is a parental advocacy group composed of determined parents with the mission of educating the state and community legislature, medical professionals, and the public as to how and why medical cannabis is available treatment for catastrophically ill children.

Our children have trialed and exhausted, on average:

  • between 10-20 invasive medication and treatments
  • including non-FDA approved drugs
  • that have never been tested on children
  • that come with considerable side effects
  • yet have failed to control their 150-300 seizures/day

We demand our legislators act swiftly to secure whole plant cannabis medicine for our children where current treatments are ineffective.
